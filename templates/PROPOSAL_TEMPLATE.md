# PROPOSAL_TEMPLATE.md
## Model Change Proposal

Conceptual changes should be proposed before modifying core documents.

## Title
Short name of the change.

## Reason for Change
What problem or inconsistency exists?

## Proposed Change
Name and definition of the concept or modification.

## Affected Documents
Which documents must change?

## Architectural Impact
How will this affect system structure?

## Migration Impact
Does the change break assumptions?

## Alternatives Considered
Other approaches and why they were rejected.

## Open Questions
Remaining uncertainties.

## Decision Status
proposed / accepted / rejected / postponed
